information Mushroom

Mushrooms are broadly categorized into four main types: saprotrophic, mycorrhizal, parasitic, and endophytic. Saprotrophic mushrooms decompose dead organic matter, mycorrhizal mushrooms form symbiotic relationships with plant roots, parasitic mushrooms grow on other living organisms, and endophytic mushrooms live inside plants. Within these broad categories, there are numerous species, some of which are edible, while others are poisonous or hallucinogenic.
